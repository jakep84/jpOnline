YouStaff

YouStaff was a hackathon project Jake won second place in. This site would connect nurses with hospitals by removing the agency so you can get the assignments you want faster and negotiate for higher wages. He built this site from a wireframe image using pure HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap.

Alpha

Alpha is an onboarding and aid tool to tutor refugees into regaining independence after they arrive to the US, specifically in terms of transportation and language skills. This app was built during the Refugee Hackathon in Atlanta using html, css, and was his first time diving into the mean stack.
